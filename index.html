The Studio

Design as a language
of living

We are not decorators. We are architects of atmosphere — translating the way you inhabit the world into physical form.

Maison Lorre was founded on a single belief: that a beautifully designed space changes how we feel, think, and live within it. For over a decade, our studio has worked with clients across Europe, the Middle East, and North America to create interiors of rare quality and enduring relevance.

Our approach is unhurried, deeply considered, and always personal. Every project begins with listening — to how you move through a space, what draws you, what calms you, what inspires you. The result is a home that feels unmistakably yours.

What We Offer

A full spectrum
of design intelligence

01
Residential Interiors

Complete interior design for private homes, apartments, and estates — from spatial planning to final installation. Each residence is conceived as a singular work.

02
Architectural Design

Working alongside architects to shape space from the inside out — wall compositions, ceiling architecture, built-in joinery, and threshold design.

03
Lighting Design

Light is the invisible material we work with most carefully. We design layered, atmospheric lighting systems that transform spaces across the arc of a day.

04
Bespoke Furniture

Custom-designed and handcrafted furniture pieces made in collaboration with master artisans. Objects that carry meaning and outlast trends.

05
Art Curation

We source and integrate original artworks as essential elements of the interior narrative — not afterthoughts, but anchors of the entire composition.

06
Hospitality & Commercial

Hotels, restaurants, private clubs, and corporate spaces designed with the same rigour as our residential work — where hospitality becomes an art form.

How We Work

A considered
journey together

01
Discovery

We begin with an unhurried conversation to understand your life, your aesthetics, and the space's potential. This is where the project truly begins.

02
Concept

We develop a comprehensive design concept — mood, palette, spatial logic, material direction — and present it as a coherent vision for your review.

03
Development

Technical drawings, sourcing, contractor coordination, bespoke commissions. We manage every complexity so the vision becomes reality with precision.

04
Installation

We oversee every aspect of the installation and final styling, ensuring that the finished space reflects the full depth of the original concept.
